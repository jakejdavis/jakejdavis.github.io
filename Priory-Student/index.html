Priory Student

The Priory Student app aims to help Priory Students by containing information like:

  • Rewards & Behavior Overview
  • To Dos
  • Timetable
  • Pastoral & Self Support
  • Student Voice
  • General School information (bulletin) all in one place.

The Priory Student app is available on different platforms:

  • iOS and Android - Hybrid Mobile App
  • Web connected devices - Web App
