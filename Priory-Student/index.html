Priory Student

Description

The one central place for students to find everything they need for school. The app contains:

  • A Rewards & Behaviour Overview
  • To Dos
  • Your Timetable
  • Wellbeing, Pastoral & Self Support
  • Student Voice
  • General School Information (bulletin)

To use this app simply login with your school username and password with no separate signup required.

The Priory Student app is being created on different platforms:

  • iOS and Android - Hybrid Mobile App
  • Web connected devices - Web App

Testing

At the moment the hybrid mobile app is in alpha and you can enroll in testing by following the instructions below:

  • iOS - You can contact me at contact@jakejdavis.me to be asked to be enrolled
  • Android - You can enroll in Android testing here
